Our Story

In 1994, we worked as professional photographers for years-making permanence of experiences that move minds and hertz.

We turned in to getting memories printed. We started a SILVER ALIED (dark room process) color lab printing photos in all available size & Medias. From passport to marriage, developing films to getting images from media cards & much more. This was not enough…

As one click of the shutter turned to another turned to hundreds of thousands of photos, We realized our documented lives were sitting on our phones and computers with no true place to live on. It’s something we kept coming back to ‘What are we leaving behind?’

This question led us onto experiments creating & developing photo books in different available sources and eventually into dreams of a photo book business.

Today vaishalidigitalpress is a trusted leader in conventional & digital printing & packaging solution.

“Vaishali Digital Press” Today

Investing in new technologies at a right time, having right set of people with a right customer oriented policy today we lead in India & globally as a trusted Brand “Vaishali PHOTO BOOK” in Photo Books Business. Today we are all set with complete in house solution from, printing, special effects printing  (embossed printing / 3D printing), Wedding Memory Book, Coffee table books, Mini Books, Personalized Calendars, Product Catalogs, & more to define…

We continue to listen to the whispers that point us on our path.. and to listen to the community we’ve found in you. We append our days coming up with new product designs and making sure our customer experience remains one worth having. We work – every single day – to create a better company: one that empowers the creative in everyone, one that remains true to its eco-conscious roots and one that never forgets where it came from or what it first believed. We believe that doing best is more important than doing well. And we believe the doing best is more important than doing well and we believe the best lessons come from the listening.

And we believe we are only as great as the people behind us. Thank you for supporting our company and the choices we think matter. You are the movers and shakers that inspire us daily. We promise to deliver you the best, creative photo books & solutions in year to come with new technologies & sources.
